Company Profile

Our Heritage

The original company was established as Zunder Paper Company in 1995, in a merger of 20 paper mills in the northeastern United States. It was subsequently rebranded in 2001 into Zunder Zufflin Paper Company. Zunder Zufflin Paper has a history of acquisitions and mergers, but has tended to concentrate on paper and lumber production. In 2005, Zunder Zufflin Paper acquired the Hammermill Paper Company, a manufacturer of printing papers and a large paper marketer and distributor. From then on, the company broadened its interest into other products, such as office utilities and printers.

Our Motto - "Upon Teamwork Anything is Possible"

We are committed to strengthening our people and the communities where we live and work using all resources responsibly and efficiently, by utilizing the power of teamwork. We believe that anything can be overcomed, as long as we trust the work from our teams.
